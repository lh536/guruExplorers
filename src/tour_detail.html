tour to huacachina & paracas full day

If you are looking for an unforgettable trip to Huacachina & Paracas, this Full Day Group tour is ideal for your next adventure. Undoubtedly, Huacachina & Paracas are one of the most outstanding tourist attractions in Peru and are able to capture the attention of more and more tourists. This tour has a duration of 16 hours where we will have many interesting activities that will offer you a different experience in Peru.

why visit huacachina & paracas?

Huacachina is an oasis in the middle of the desert a few hours from Lima. It is a place surrounded by sand dunes where many people practice adventure sports and sports attractions with tubular cars through the sandy mountains. It also offers alternatives such as traditional wine and Pisco wineries and walks with spectacular views.

Paracas is another unforgettable destination that will make this tour a complete experience. It is one of Peru’s favorite destinations. By many, considered as the best, something that is due to all the activities you can find in this place and what you can do when you visit it. In addition to its extensive beaches and expanses of reserve, you can taste the best cuisine because it is a place where you can eat the best fish in the country.

In short, if you need to make a different trip, where you can enjoy varied experiences that combine landscape, adventure, gastronomy, culture and nature, then you should not wait any longer. The Huacachina & Paracas Full Day Tour is waiting for you with its excellent group service.

itinerary
Route

Huacachina & Paracas Full Day Tour Itinerary (Group Service):

5:00 – 9:30 hs Departure to Paracas – Arrival at the Ballestas Islands

We leave very early from Paracas from a chosen concentration site and we will make a 4-hour trip in our transport. After arriving at the port of Paracas “El Chaco”, we will visit the Ballestas Islands to observe the famous candelabra, an enigmatic geoglyph 180 m long and 1.2 m deep that can only be seen from the sea or the air because of its location.

We continue our tour to the Ballesta Islands where we will be able to appreciate its marine fauna, such as large colonies of guano birds, Humboldt penguins, the friendly sea lions and with a little luck we will also be able to see dolphins. This tour has a 2 hours duration.

13:00 – 14:30 hs Transportation – Tour of the City of Ica

After arriving in the city of Ica, we will enjoy a delicious lunch at the Catador hacienda with a menu included in the tour. Then, we will make the excursion to its winery where grape brandy, known as Pisco, is made. In addition, you will visit the old winery Fundo Tres Esquinas, which has been in operation since 1856, where you will observe the entire process of making wine and Pisco with the guide of an experienced worker of the winery.

We will also find antique pieces such as clay pots, distillation devices, acacia press (huarango).

Once the visit ends, we will enjoy tastings of the products (wines, piscos, pisco creams and mistelles), accompanied with typical dynamic phrases of the city of Ica.

14:30 – 16:00 hs Transporte – Recorrida por Huacachina

Once the visit to the hacienda is over, we will start the trip to the oasis of Huacachina, just 5 kilometers from the center of the city of Ica. Here we will find a beautiful lagoon where you will enjoy the tranquility of the site, walk, take pictures and do adventure sports such as Buggies and Sandboarding.

16:00 – 18:30 hs Viaje hacia Chicha – Bodega Racimo de Uva

Once we have enjoyed the tour of Huacachina, we will leave Ica towards Chincha to the Racimo de Uva Winery to make a brief stop and finish the tour in the best way.

18:30 – 22:00 hs Retorno a Lima

Having finished this tour, we will start our trip to Lima, arriving at our destination around 10:00 pm.
